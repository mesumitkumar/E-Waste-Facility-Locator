About Us


Welcome to E-Waste Facility Locator, your trusted partner in responsible e-waste disposal and environmental sustainability. At E-Waste Facility Locator, we are passionate about making a positive impact on the environment by helping individuals and businesses dispose of electronic waste in an eco-friendly and responsible manner.

Our Mission:

Our mission is to raise awareness about the harmful effects of improper e-waste disposal and provide users with the tools they need to make a difference.

Who We Are:

We are a dedicated team of environmental enthusiasts, tech experts, and sustainability advocates who believe in the power of technology to drive positive change.

What We Do:

We provide a comprehensive platform that connects you with the nearest e-waste collection and recycling facilities, making it easy for you to make responsible choices when it comes to disposing of your old electronics.

Why Choose Us:

We are committed to promoting sustainability and responsible e-waste management. We offer educational resources to help you understand the environmental and health impacts of e-waste. We reward your responsible actions by giving you credits for recycling your old devices. Join us in our mission to protect the planet and create a more sustainable future. Together, we can make a difference, one electronic device at a time.

Our Services

At E-Waste Facility Locator, we offer a range of services to help you with responsible e-waste disposal and environmental education. Here's what you can expect from our platform:

1. E-Waste Facility Locator:

Easily find the nearest e-waste collection and recycling facilities in your area. Access detailed information about each facility, including location, contact details, operating hours, and materials accepted.

2. Educational Resources:

Learn about the harmful components of e-waste and their effects on the environment and human health. Get valuable tips on how to dispose of e-waste safely and responsibly.

3. Device Estimation and Credit Points:

Input details about your old electronic devices to estimate the recyclable materials they contain. Earn credits based on the estimated value of the recyclable materials, which you can redeem for rewards.

4. User Profiles:

Create and manage user profiles to track your e-waste disposal history and earned credits. Stay informed about your contributions to environmental causes.

5. Environmental Impact Calculator:

Calculate and visualize the positive environmental impact of your responsible e-waste disposal actions. See how your choices reduce carbon footprints and benefit the planet.

6. News and Updates:

Stay up to date with the latest news, articles, and developments in e-waste management and sustainability. Explore informative content to expand your knowledge.

7. Facility Reviews and Ratings:

Share your experiences with e-waste collection and recycling facilities by leaving reviews and ratings. Help others make informed decisions and improve facility transparency.

8. Resource and Partnership Access:

Access a curated list of resources, links, and partnerships related to e-waste regulations, recycling programs, and eco-friendly electronics. Join us in our mission to protect the environment, raise awareness, and reward responsible e-waste disposal. Together, we can create a more sustainable future for generations to come.
